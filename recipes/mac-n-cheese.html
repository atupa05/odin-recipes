Mac N Cheese

Mac and cheese is a classic comfort food made with tender pasta coated in a rich, creamy cheese sauce. It's quick to prepare and perfect as a side dish or a satisfying main meal. This version keeps it simple and uses a stovetop method, so no baking is required. You can customize it with extra seasonings, toppings, or different cheeses to suit your taste.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the macaroni and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make a roux: In the same pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, whisk in the flour and cook for 1â€“2 minutes, stirring constantly to form a smooth paste
  3. Add milk: Slowly pour in the milk while whisking to prevent lumps. Continue cooking and stirring until the mixture thickens slightly
  4. Add cheese: Reduce heat to low and stir in the shredded cheddar cheese. Mix until completely melted and smooth.
  5. Combine pasta and sauce: Add the drained pasta to the cheese sauce and stir until all the noodles are evenly coated.
  6. Season: Taste and season with salt and black pepper as needed. Serve hot and enjoy!
