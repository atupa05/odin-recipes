Peanut Butter & Jelly

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a classic sandwich.

The sandwich contains two slices of bread with two delictible spreads on the inside

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Preferred flavor of jelly
  • Butter knife

Instructions:

  1. Lay out the two slices of bread separately
  2. Open the peanut butter
  3. Grab the handle butter knife
  4. Use the end of the butter knife with ridges to scoop peanut butter out of the jar
  5. spread the peanutbutter evenly on one slice of bread
  6. Open the jelly
  7. Use the end of the butter knife with ridges to scoop jelly out of the jar
  8. spread jelly on the slice of bread without peanutbutter on it
  9. Put down the butter knife in the sink
  10. Put the two slices of break together so the applied peanut butter and jelly are touching

Enjoy!
