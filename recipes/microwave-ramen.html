Microwave ramen is a quick and easy meal you can make in under 5 minutes. It’s perfect when you want a warm, filling dish with minimal cleanup. Using instant ramen noodles, you just add water, microwave, and mix in the seasoning for a fast snack or meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack instant ramen
  • 2 cups water
  • Seasoning packet
  • Optional:sliced green onions, soy sauce, or egg for extra flavor

Instructinos:

  1. Place the ramen block in a large microwave-safe bowl
  2. Pour in water until the noodles are fully submerged
  3. Microwave on high for 3-4 minutes
  4. Carefully remove the bowl and stir the noodles
  5. Drain some water if you prefer less broth
  6. Add the Seasoning packet and stir until dissolved
  7. (Optional) Top with green onions, soy sauce, or a cracked egg for extra flavor.
  8. Let sit for 1–2 minutes to cool slightly before eating.
